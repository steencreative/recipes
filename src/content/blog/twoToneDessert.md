## Two Tone Dessert

1 c. flour
½ c. butter
½ c. chopped nuts
              ~ Press in 9x13 pan – Bake 350° - 10 min.

Cream: (first layer)
              8 oz. cream cheese
              1 c. pd. Sugar
              2/3 pt. whipped whipping cream – fold in
              (2nd layer)
              2 small pkgs. Pudding – 2 1/3 c. milk

Top with 1/3 pt. whipped whipping cream (sweeten cream)
