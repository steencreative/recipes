## Earthquake Cake

Sprinkle in bottom of greased 9x13 pan
              1 c. pecans
              1 c. cocout
German choc. Cake mix – make according to directions
Pour over nuts/coconut
Mix and drop by spoonfuls on top of cake
              8 oz. cream cheese
              ½ c. butter
              1½ c. pd. Sugar
              1 t. vanilla
Bake 350° for 45 minutes (mom bakes 50-55 minutes)
