## Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

From the Kitchen of:  Sue Denney
1 c. Crisco
1 c. white sugar
1 c. brown sugar
              ~ cream
2 eggs (add to the above 1 @ a time)
½ t. vanilla
2 c. flour
½ t. salt
½ t. b. powder
1 t. baking soda
2 c. oatmeal
1 c. choc. Chips
Bake 350°, 10 - 12 min.
