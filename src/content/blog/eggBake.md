## Egg Bake

Serves:  12
9 x 13 greased pan
8 slices bread cut in 1 “ cubes (line in pan)
Mix and pour over: 
              A little less than 1 lb. valveta cheese
              8 eggs – beaten
              4 c. milk
              2 t. dry mustard
              1 drop tobasco sauce
              1 t. salt
              Sprinkle w/ fried bacon
Cover and refrigerate overnight
Let stand 10 minutes. 
Bake 350° - 1 hr.
