## Pound Cake

### Ingredients

2 ¼ c. flour
2 c. sugar
½ t. salt
½ t. soda
1 t. lemon peal
1 t. vanilla
1 c. butter
1 c. sour cream
3 eggs
 
### Directions

Grease and Flour 10" Bundt pan
Beat low speed 3 minutes
Bake 325° for 60-70 minutes. 
Cool in pan 15 minutes upright
