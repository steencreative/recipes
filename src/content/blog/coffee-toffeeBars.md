## Coffee-Toffee Bars

2 c. flour
1 c. butter
½ t. b. powder
1 c. br. Sugar
1-2 T. instant coffee
¼ t. salt
1 t. almond
1 c. choc. Chips
 
Greased 9x13 pan.  Bake 350° for 20-25 minutes. 
 
Icing:        1 T. butter
              ¾ c. pd. Sugar
              1/8 t. almond
              1 – 2 T. milk
