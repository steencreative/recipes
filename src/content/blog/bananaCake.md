Banana Cake

9x13 pan, grease-floured
Sift:  2 c. flour
       ½ t. b. powder
       ¾ t. b. soda
       ½ t. salt
Mix (1½ min.):  ½ c. shortening
                1½ c. sugar
                2 eggs
                1 t. vanilla
2-3 bananas – add
¼ c. buttermilk
alt. milk and flour – beat 2 min.
