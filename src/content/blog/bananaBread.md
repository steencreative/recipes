## Banana Bread

Bake 350° - 1 hr. 15 min. – 9 “ loaf – low rack
Cream:  ½ c. shortening ( ¼ c. butter, ¼ c. oil)
        1 c. sugar
        2 eggs
Dissolve:  1 t. soda
           3 T. hot water
3 mashed bananas (1 ½ cups)
2 c. flour
¼ t. salt
