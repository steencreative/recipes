## Garlic Bread

1 loaf Italian Bread
½ c. soft butter
1 t. parsley flakes
¼ t. oregano
1 clove garlic – minced
 
Spread on Bread – Sprinkle w/ parm. Cheese and parsley
Put in oven foil.  Bake 400° 10 min.
